Privacy Policy

Janderson Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026  ·  Operated by: Jason Anderson

This Privacy Policy explains how Janderson (“we,” “us,” or the “Service”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the SMS verification service operated by Jason Anderson. The Service sends one-time passcodes (OTPs) and login verification codes to the account owner for authentication purposes only.

01 Information we collect

We collect a single category of personal information: the mobile phone number that the account owner enters and verifies within the application. We also generate and process the verification codes sent to that number, along with basic delivery metadata (timestamp and delivery status) needed to operate the Service.

We do not collect names, payment information, location data, or any other personal information through this messaging program.

02 How we use information

The mobile number is used solely to deliver one-time passcodes and login verification codes that authenticate the account owner. These are transactional messages. We do not use the number for marketing, promotional, or informational messaging of any kind.

03 How we share information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share the mobile number only with our messaging infrastructure provider (Twilio) strictly to transmit the verification codes you have requested. That provider processes the data solely to deliver messages on our behalf.

SMS consent and opt-in data

No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties or affiliates under any circumstances.

04 Message frequency, rates, and opting out

Message frequency varies and is triggered only when a login or verification event requires a code. Message and data rates may apply. You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at the address below.

05 Data retention and security

Verification codes are short-lived and expire shortly after they are issued. We retain the mobile number only for as long as the account owner maintains the account, and delivery metadata only as long as needed to operate and troubleshoot the Service. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect this information against unauthorized access.

06 Your choices

Because the recipient is the account owner, you may remove your mobile number or disable SMS verification at any time within the application, or by replying STOP. You may also contact us to request deletion of your number from our records.

07 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

08 Contact

Questions about this policy or the Service can be sent to jason@andersonfamilyventures.com.
